# PROJECT INTENT: SYF V4 REDESIGN (ATOMIC HUD)

## 🎯 Goal
Redesign the current syf-manager from a "low-fidelity functional HUD" to a "SOTA High-Fidelity Atomic Digital Artifact". The goal is to create an experience that feels like a top-tier science fiction interface from the year 2026/2077, blending Retro-Futurism, Cyberpunk, and Pixel Art into a singular, premium "5 atomówek" aesthetic.

## 👥 Target Persona
**Paulina Janowska (The Guardian/Creator)**
- Visionary user who demands visual excellence.
- Needs a "command center" that doesn't just work, but *commands* presence.
- Values: Density of information, premium animations, brutalist precision, and aesthetic dominance.

## 🛠 Use Case
- Managing AI syf (files, logs, artifacts).
- Monitoring system vitals.
- Interacting with Gniewisława in a high-intensity environment.

## 🎭 Mood & Atmosphere
- **5 ATOMÓWEK**: Aggressive, radioactive, intense, glowing.
- **NEO-HUD**: Military-grade meets elite hacker aesthetic.
- **KINETIC**: Everything moves. Scanlines, glitches, micro-interactions.
- **INDUSTRIAL**: Heavy borders, pixel-perfect alignment, mono-fonts.

## 🧱 Constraints
- Stack: Next.js 16 (App Router), Tailwind CSS.
- High Performance: Must maintain high FPS despite heavy visual effects.
- No placeholders: All assets must be premium.
