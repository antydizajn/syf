# RESEARCH: AGI Ecosystem Design

## 🔬 Industry Benchmarks
1. **OpenAI (openai.com)**: Clean, monochrome, massive type, focus on "Safety" and "Capability."
2. **Anthropic (anthropic.com)**: Editorial style, serif + mono pairing, high contrast, focus on "Humanity" and "Research."
3. **Vercel (vercel.com)**: Developer-centric, high-performance UI (Framework focus), black/white/neon.
4. **Mistral AI**: Simple, clean, tech-first, raw power presentation.

## 🎨 Creative Direction for Antigravity
- **Background**: Deep Graphite/OLED Black (`#000101`).
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk (Sci-fi professional) + Inter (Modern/Readable).
- **accents**: Phosphor Green (`#39FF14`) for data, Cyber Red (`#FF003C`) for critical modules.
- **Texture**: Subtle dither patterns and scanlines to preserve "Antidizajn" DNA but in a refined form.

## 📐 UX Patterns
- **Grid Supremacy**: Everything locked to an 8px grid.
- **Dynamic HUD**: Real-time stats and metrics in borders (Simulated or Real).
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first scalability.
